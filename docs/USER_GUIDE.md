## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Navigate to the login page

## Inventory Management

### Managing Stock Items

#### View Stock Items
1. Navigate to **Inventory > Stock Items**
2. Use filters to find specific items
3. Click on any item for detailed view

#### Add New Stock Item
1. Click "Add Stock Item" button
2. Fill in required fields:
   - Item Code (unique identifier)
   - Name and Description
   - Category (Equipment, Spare Parts, etc.)
   - Unit of measurement
   - Unit price
   - Reorder level
   - Initial quantity
3. Click "Create Item"

#### Record Stock Movement
1. Navigate to **Inventory > Movements**
2. Click "Record Movement"
3. Select:
   - Item from dropdown
   - Movement type (Stock In, Stock Out, etc.)
   - Quantity
   - Reason/Notes
4. Click "Record"

#### Low Stock Alerts
- Items below reorder level show in red
- Dashboard displays low stock count
- Automatic procurement suggestions via AI

### Warehouse Management
1. Navigate to **Inventory > Warehouses**
2. View all warehouse locations
3. Add new warehouses as needed
4. Assign items to warehouses

## Asset Management

### Managing Assets

#### View Assets
1. Navigate to **Assets**
2. Filter by status or category
3. Click asset for full details

#### Register New Asset
1. Click "Add Asset"
2. Enter details:
   - Asset code and name
   - Category (Vehicle, Equipment, etc.)
   - Purchase information
   - Current location
   - Status
3. Click "Register Asset"

#### Schedule Maintenance
1. Open asset details
2. Click "Schedule Maintenance"
3. Select:
   - Maintenance type
   - Scheduled date
   - Description
4. Click "Schedule"

#### Track Asset History
- View maintenance history
- See value depreciation
- Track location changes
- Review status updates

## Finance Management

### Budget Management

#### Create Budget
1. Navigate to **Finance > Budgets**
2. Click "Create Budget"
3. Fill in:
   - Budget name
   - Category
   - Allocated amount
   - Department
   - Fiscal period
4. Click "Create"

#### Track Budget Utilization
- View spent vs. allocated amounts
- Monitor utilization percentage
- Get alerts when near budget limit
- Review expense breakdown by category

### Expense Management

#### Submit Expense
1. Navigate to **Finance > Expenses**
2. Click "Submit Expense"
3. Provide:
   - Description
   - Amount
   - Category
   - Budget allocation
   - Receipt (upload)
4. Submit for approval

#### Approve Expenses
*For managers and finance officers*
1. Navigate to **Approvals > Expenses**
2. Review expense details
3. Approve or reject with comments

### Payments
1. Navigate to **Finance > Payments**
2. View all payment records
3. Track payment status
4. Generate payment reports

## HR & Personnel

### Employee Management

#### Add Employee
1. Navigate to **HR > Employees**
2. Click "Add Employee"
3. Enter employee details:
   - Personal information
   - Position and department
   - Salary and benefits
   - Start date
4. Click "Create"

#### Record Attendance
1. Navigate to **HR > Attendance**
2. Click "Record Attendance"
3. Select employee and date
4. Enter check-in/check-out times
5. Mark status (Present, Absent, Late, etc.)

### Leave Management

#### Request Leave
1. Navigate to **HR > Leave Requests**
2. Click "Request Leave"
3. Fill in:
   - Leave type (Annual, Sick, etc.)
   - Start and end dates
   - Reason
4. Submit for approval

#### Approve Leave Requests
*For HR managers*
1. View pending requests
2. Review employee leave balance
3. Approve or reject with comments

### Recruitment

#### Post Job Opening
1. Navigate to **HR > Recruitment > Jobs**
2. Click "Post Job"
3. Use AI to generate job description
4. Review and publish

#### Screen Candidates
1. Navigate to **HR > Recruitment > Screening**
2. Upload candidate CV
3. AI parses and analyzes qualifications
4. Review AI screening results
5. Shortlist or reject

## Safety & Compliance

### Safety Inspections

#### Schedule Inspection
1. Navigate to **Safety > Inspections**
2. Click "Schedule Inspection"
3. Enter:
   - Inspection type
   - Location
   - Date and inspector
   - Checklist items
4. Schedule

#### Complete Inspection
1. Open scheduled inspection
2. Go through checklist
3. Mark Pass/Fail for each item
4. Add findings and recommendations
5. Submit report

### Safety Training

#### Schedule Training
1. Navigate to **Safety > Training**
2. Click "Schedule Training"
3. Fill in:
   - Title and type
   - Date and duration
   - Instructor
   - Max participants
4. Schedule and notify employees

### Certifications
1. View all certifications in **Safety > Certifications**
2. Monitor expiring certifications (30-day alerts)
3. Renew certifications before expiry
4. Upload certificate documents

### Safety Drills
1. Navigate to **Safety > Drills**
2. Schedule drills by type
3. Record drill evaluations
4. Track performance ratings
5. Review strengths and weaknesses

## Best Practices

### Inventory
- Update stock levels immediately after movements
- Set appropriate reorder levels
- Use barcode scanning when available
- Regular stock counts and reconciliation

### Finance
- Submit expenses with receipts promptly
- Review budget utilization weekly
- Keep budget categories aligned with accounting
- Regular financial reviews

### HR
- Record attendance daily
- Process leave requests within 48 hours
- Keep employee information updated
- Schedule regular performance reviews

### Safety
- Complete inspections on schedule
- Address failed inspections immediately
- Keep certifications current
- Conduct regular safety drills
- Report incidents promptly
